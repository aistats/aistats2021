Volunteers and Travel Support

Volunteers

Only students are allowed to volunteer. Selected volunteers will be asked to help on 3-hour shifts. The tasks will include works at the registration desk, support during the oral/poster presentation, and possibly others. We will waive the registration fees for the volunteers.

Travel Support

Travel support will be awarded to a limited number of student presenters based on quality and need.

Application

To apply, please send an email to with the following information:

  • Student ID and supervisor name
  • Your CV
  • Cover letter
  • Type of application: volunteer, travel support, or both
  • List of accepted papers to AISTATS 2016

Application deadline: Jan 20, 2016

In order to know the number of volunteers needed, we will need to know the number of conference registrants. Therefore, students who applied for volunteers and/or travel support are encouraged to register before Feb 8, 2016 to get an early-bird discount. If you are selected as a volunteer, your registration fee will be fully refunded.
