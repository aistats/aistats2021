The {{ site.conference.instance }} International Conference on
{{site.conference.full_name }}

April 13 - 15
San Diego, California and Online

AISTATS 2021 will be an asynchronous virtual conference where participants will have access to the videos of the keynotes and of the papers to be watched at any time in a virtual webpage. In addition, we will use a chat system in which participants can ask questions in written form to the keynote speakers and to authors. Keynote speakers and authors will also be available for two live Q&A sessions. The videos will be accessible to all participants from around one week before the conference starts, and to everyone else after the conference.
Registration will be open until April 13th at AISTATS 2021 Registration (€40 Students, €60 Non-students).

Since its inception in 1985, AISTATS has been an interdisciplinary gathering of researchers at the intersection of artificial intelligence, machine learning, statistics, and related areas.
