AISTATS 2021 will be an asynchronous virtual conference where participants will have access to the videos of the keynotes and of the papers to be watched at any time in a virtual webpage. In addition, we will use a chat system in which participants can ask questions in written form to the keynote speakers and to authors. Keynote speakers and authors will also be available for two live Q and A sessions. The videos will be accessible to all participants from around one week before the conference starts, and to everyone else after the conference.
